🚀 Best Practices for AI Prompting

Click each section to explore key strategies for writing effective AI prompts.

📌 Unambiguous Prompts

Clearly define the desired response to avoid misinterpretation. Be specific about what you want (summary vs. analysis, etc.).

🧠 Adequate Context

Include context and specify format. Example: "List 5 movies from the 1990s in a table."

⚖️ Balanced Prompts

Avoid prompts that are too vague or too complex. Keep them clear and manageable.

🔁 Experiment & Refine

Test and revise prompts. Iteration improves clarity and results.
